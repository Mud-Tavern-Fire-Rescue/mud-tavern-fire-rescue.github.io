VOLUNTEER WITH US

{% endcomment %}

Mud Tavern Volunteer Fire and Rescue has 1 rescue truck, 1 quick response vehicle, and one emergency operations trailer.

We have general business meetings every first and third Thursday of the month. Training is every second and fourth Thursday of the month. Meetings and training start at 7:00 pm. If you are interested in learning more, we invite you to stop by on Thursday night. Someone will be able to talk to you about what you can expect as a volunteer.
